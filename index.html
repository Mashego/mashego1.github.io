About usWelcome to Ama-Nutti Peanut Butter!

Provides training to domestic workers in the criteria of wholesome home cooked meals. Training on a one on one basis, makes it easy & fun while learning. Each menu will be taught on a step by step basis. Classes are performed in a home kitchen, with the maximum of 2 trainees per lesson. The course runs for 2 days, starting from 9am - 3pm. The 2 day course is compiled of the following:

  • We use raw Peanuts
  • We make smooth Peanut Butter .
  • We make crunchy Peanut Butter

Been in the catering business for over 12 years & training kitchen staff with excellent cooking skills, has brought the desire to teach people skills they never knew they had. Also giving you peace of mind knowing that your employee is skilled in this criteria & coming home to a fabulous meal.

There are 2 menus to choose from.Each menu done at the cost of R2500.

We also do Platterss for any occasion and ready frozen meals.
